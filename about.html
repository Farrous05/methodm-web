About MethodM

Built on Methodology. Proven By Results.

MethodM is a specialist F&B and hospitality consultancy founded in Beirut, Lebanon, in 2013. We exist to bring financial clarity, operational precision, and genuine partnership to hospitality operators across Lebanon, Cyprus, and EMEA.

Who We Are

MethodM Business Consultants is a specialist F&B and hospitality consultancy based in Beirut, Lebanon. Founded in 2013 by Maya and Mike, MethodM has served 56 brands and 460 outlets across Lebanon, Cyprus, and the EMEA region.

We specialize in five integrated disciplines: cost control & inventory management, financial accounting, purchasing optimization, system assessment, and consultancy & training. Our methodology is built around accuracy, clarity, and transparency.

We don't just advise — we work inside your operation. We cross-validate every number, execute alongside your team, and treat your business as a shared responsibility. That is why 25% of our clients came to us from a previous consultant. And that is why they stay.
