Privacy Policy

How MethodM collects, uses, and protects information you share with us.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

MethodM (Method M Business Consultants) is a F&B and hospitality consultancy based in Beirut, Lebanon, serving clients across Lebanon, Cyprus, and EMEA. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal data collected through this website.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the following:

  • Your full name
  • Your company or concept name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • The contents of your message (optional free-text field)
  • Whether you have requested a meeting

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics at this time. If we add analytics tools in the future, this policy will be updated and disclosed prominently.

3. How We Use Your Data

The information you submit is used solely to respond to your inquiry and, if relevant, to discuss potential engagement with MethodM. We do not use your data for marketing automation or unsolicited outreach.

4. How Your Data Is Stored

Form submissions are transmitted by email to the MethodM team via a serverless function hosted on Vercel. We do not maintain a database of website inquiries. The receiving inbox is accessed only by MethodM staff.

5. Who We Share Data With

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. The only entities involved in the technical handling of your submission are:

  • Our hosting provider (Vercel), which processes the form submission in transit.
  • Our transactional email provider, used to deliver the submission to our inbox.

Both providers are bound by their own data-processing agreements and applicable data-protection laws.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and equivalent regulations, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@methodmlb.com or call +961 3 711 009. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain inquiry emails only as long as necessary to handle the conversation and any subsequent engagement. Emails relating to inquiries that do not result in a working relationship are deleted within 24 months.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is intended for business professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 16.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
