## Why we added a Privacy page

This wasn't in the original list of issues, but the SEO/GEO audit flagged it as a **high-severity, pre-launch requirement** and it's also a legal requirement:

1. **Legal compliance (GDPR)** — The contact form collects personal information (name, company, phone, email, message). Because MethodM serves clients in Cyprus, an EU country, EU privacy law (GDPR) applies. Collecting personal data without a published Privacy Policy is technically a violation and exposes the business to risk.
2. **Trust signal** — Google factors the presence of a Privacy Policy into how trustworthy it considers a website. Sites without one rank lower.
3. **Visitor trust** — Decision-makers visiting a consultancy site notice the absence of a Privacy Policy. It's a small thing that quietly signals professionalism.

The page explains, in plain English, what data is collected, what we do with it, who it's shared with (nobody — only used to reply to inquiries), and how someone can ask for their data to be deleted. It also references Lebanon and Cyprus / EMEA explicitly. The footer of every page now has a "Privacy" link.

2. **Founders' full surnames** — currently displayed as "Maya K." and "Mike E." Full names are needed for both visible text and the data Google uses to identify them. If they have public LinkedIn profiles, those help too.
3. **Team members (14 people)** — the About page has 16 team cards. Maya and Mike are real; the other 14 are placeholders. For each person we need:
   - Full name and job title
   - A professional headshot (consistent style across all 16)
   - A short quote (under 15 words, in their own voice)
   - If photos aren't ready by launch, the current colored-initial avatars are perfectly fine to keep.
4. **Real testimonials** — currently the testimonials show role-only attributions like "Operations Manager — Multi-outlet Restaurant Group, Beirut." For launch we need at least 3 testimonials with named clients, including:

The plan is to replace it with a greyscale collage of real MethodM client spaces / restaurant interiors / team in operation. Need 6–12 high-resolution photos with permission to use. If they're not available, the current dark hero stays.
7. **Decision on the "MethodM Intelligence" preview** on the homepage. Right now it shows a stylized fake dashboard. Three options:

8. **A branded social-share image** (1200×630 pixels): MethodM logo on a dark background with the tagline "Hospitality Success Makers." This is what shows up as the preview image when the site is shared on WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Slack, etc.
9. **HR Solutions brief** — needed to flesh out the placeholder HR tab on the Services page. We need:
